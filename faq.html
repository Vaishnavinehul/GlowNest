Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

What does “Clean Compatible” mean for GlowNest products?

“Clean Compatible” means we don’t just avoid harmful or toxic chemicals — every ingredient is chosen to be both safe and beneficial. Our formulations aim to improve the health of your skin and hair, not just serve beauty.

Are your products tested for safety?

Yes — our products are derma-tested, India FDA-approved, and made without animal testing. They follow strict quality and safety standards.

How long will it take to see results?

It depends on the product and your skin/hair type. For things like brightening or peeling, you might see visible changes in a few weeks. Hair growth serums might take longer (often several weeks) to show noticeable improvement.

What is your return / refund policy?

We accept returns or refunds under certain conditions (defective products, wrong shipments, etc.). Please see our Refund Policy and Shipping & Returns pages for full details on eligibility and procedure.

How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you’ll receive a confirmation and tracking details. You can also track your order via our “Track your Order” section on the website.
