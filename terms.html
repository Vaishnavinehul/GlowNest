Your product Order

When you place an order with GlowNest, you're embarking on a journey to bring a little extra glow into your life!
Here's what you can expect regarding your product orders:

Step-1: Ordering Process

Browsing & Selection: Explore our range of products on www.glownest.com. Add your desired items to your cart.
Checkout: Once you're ready, proceed to checkout. You'll be asked to provide shipping and payment information.
Order Confirmation: After placing your order, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details. Please review this carefully and contact us immediately if you notice any discrepancies.

Step-2: Shipping & Delivery

Domestic Shipping: We currently deliver within India. Shipping rates are calculated based on the total weight of your order.
Processing Time: As many of our products are custom-made, please allow 7-10 business days for domestic shipments. We do not deliver on Saturdays, Sundays, or public holidays.
Tracking: You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Step-3: Returns & Refunds

Damaged or Incorrect Items: If you receive a product in a bad condition or if the packaging is tampered with, please refuse to accept the delivery and return the package to the delivery person. Contact us immediately, and we will ensure a brand new replacement is issued at no additional cost.
Return Eligibility: For returns, the product must be in unused condition, with original tags and packaging intact. There should be no signs of wear and tear.
Non-Refundable Items: Shipping charges and Cash on Delivery (COD) charges are non-refundable.
Refund Processing: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 days of receiving the returned product.
Payment: We accept various payment methods for your convenience. Please refer to the checkout page for available options.
