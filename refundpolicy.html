Refund Policy

Wrong Item Delivered

1. Do not open or use the wrong item. Keep the product in its original packaging, with all seals, labels, and tags intact. Using the product may complicate the return process.

2. Document everything. Take clear photos or a video of the package and the item you received. Make sure the shipping label with the order number and your address is visible. This serves as proof for your claim.

3. Find your order details. Locate your order confirmation email or log into your account on the GlowNest website to find your order number and the details of the item you originally purchased.

Item Damaged

GlowNest's policy is very specific for this situation:

"If you receive a product in a bad condition or if the packaging is tampered with, please refuse to accept the delivery and return the package to the delivery person. Contact us immediately, and we will ensure a brand new replacement is issued at no additional cost."

This is the most direct and effective way to resolve the issue according to their terms. By refusing the delivery, you are immediately signaling to both the courier and the company that the item is not in acceptable condition.
